Cyber Security overview

We had the cybersecurity session for 4 days.
The key difference between information security and cybersecurity i.e. Info security deals with all types of data while cybersecurity deals mainly with digital data

  • Integrity
  • Confidentiality
  • Availabilty
These are all part of the CIA triad.
Types of data, various types of attacks, cyber killer chain, types of risks their assessment and management, traffic analysis were all part of the foundational concepts we were introduced to.
for the practical sessions we got introduced to man-in-the-middle attacks and cross-site-scripting
